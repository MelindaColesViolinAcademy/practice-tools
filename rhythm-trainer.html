Clap first. Count aloud. Then play on an open string.

Ta Ta Ta Ta
1 2 3 4

How to Practise

  • 👏 Clap the rhythm.
  • 🗣 Count out loud.
  • 🎻 Play on an open string.
  • 🎵 Repeat until it feels easy.
  • 🥁 Then try it in D Major.

Beginner Tips

  • Slow is good.
  • Keep a steady pulse.
  • Use long relaxed bows.
  • Listen carefully.
  • Accuracy before speed.
